## 1. Platform Overview

**NUVRA** is a football management platform with two distinct portals operating under one roof:

| Portal | Purpose | Who Uses It |
|---|---|---|
| **Club Portal** | Professional team management — track squad performance, schedules, and payments | Coaches and registered team players |
| **Community Portal** | Recreational / grassroots football — find, join, and pay for community pickup games | Community players and community admins |

Both portals are accessible from the same web application. Users register and log in separately for each portal.

---

## 2. User Roles at a Glance

| Role | Portal | Key Abilities |
|---|---|---|
| **Coach** | Club | Manage squad, log match stats, post announcements, manage schedule, view team payments |
| **Player** | Club | View own performance, see announcements & schedule, make monthly subscription payments |
| **Pending Player** | Club | Awaiting coach approval after registration; limited access |
| **Community Player** | Community | Browse games, join teams, submit payment receipts |
| **Community Admin** | Community | Create games, post community announcements, view analytics |

## 3. Getting Started

### 3.1 Club Portal — Registration & Login

**Accessing the Club Portal**

Navigate to the main NUVRA website. The default landing page is the Club Portal.

---

**Registering as a New Player**

1. Click **Register** on the login page.
2. Enter your **name**, **email address**, and **password**.
3. Click **Sign Up**.
4. You will be redirected to the **Onboarding** page where you must complete your player profile:
   - Select your **team** (which coach/team you belong to)
   - Choose your **playing position** (Goalkeeper, Defender, Midfielder, Forward)
   - Enter your **date of birth**
   - Enter your **jersey number**
5. After submitting, your account status will be **Pending** — your coach must approve you before you gain full access.
6. You will see a **Waiting Room** page while your approval is being processed.

> **Note:** You can also register and log in using your **Google account** by clicking **Continue with Google**. New Google users will still go through the onboarding step above.

---

**Logging In as a Returning User**

1. Enter your registered **email** and **password**.
2. Click **Login**.
3. You will be directed to your dashboard based on your role (Coach or Player).

---

**Forgot Password**

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link.
4. Follow the link to set a new password.

---

### 3.2 Community Portal — Registration & Login

**Accessing the Community Portal**

From the main NUVRA site, navigate to the **Community** section (separate from the Club Portal).

**Registering**

1. Click **Register** on the Community login page.
2. Provide your **name**, **email**, and **password**.
3. Click **Sign Up** — you will be taken directly to the Community feed (no approval needed).

**Logging In**

1. Enter your **email** and **password** on the Community login page.
2. Click **Login** to access the Community game feed.

## 4. Club Portal — Coach

After logging in, coaches land on the **Coach Dashboard**. The left sidebar provides navigation to all coaching tools.

---

### 4.1 Squad Management

**What it does:** Displays all players registered to your team, including their profile details, position, jersey number, and current approval status.

**Key actions:**
- **View player profiles** — click any player to see their full profile, attributes, and performance history.
- **Approve or decline pending players** — new players who selected your team during onboarding will appear here with a **Pending** badge. Click their profile and choose **Approve** or **Decline**.
- **View player status** — Active, Pending.

---

### 4.2 Recording Player Stats

**What it does:** Allows coaches to log individual player performance for each match.

**How to record stats:**

1. Navigate to **Record Stats** in the sidebar.
2. Select the **player** from the squad list.
3. Fill in match details:
   - Opponent name
   - Match date and time
   - Venue
   - League / event type
4. Fill in player performance:
   - Goals scored
   - Assists
   - Minutes played
   - Performance rating (0–10 scale)
   - Clean sheet (Yes / No — relevant for goalkeepers and defenders)
5. Click **Save** to record the stats.

These stats feed directly into each player's performance dashboard and analytics charts.

---

### 4.3 Announcements

**What it does:** Allows coaches to post team-wide notices visible to all squad players.

**How to post an announcement:**

1. Navigate to **Announcements** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New Announcement**.
3. Enter a **title** and the announcement **content/body**.
4. Click **Post** — all active players on your team will see it immediately on their dashboard.

You can also **delete** old announcements from the same screen.

---

### 4.4 Schedule & Calendar

**What it does:** Displays and manages upcoming matches and training sessions in a calendar view.

**How to add a schedule event:**

1. Navigate to **Schedule** in the sidebar.
2. Click a date on the calendar or use the **Add Event** button.
3. Enter event details:
   - Event title (e.g., "Training Session" or "Match vs XFC")
   - Date and time
   - Venue / location
   - Category (Match / Training)
4. Save the event — it will appear on the coach's calendar and on every active player's schedule view.

---

### 4.5 Payment Overview

**What it does:** Gives coaches a summary of which players have paid their monthly subscription fee.

**How to use:**

1. Navigate to **Payment** in the sidebar.
2. Select a **month** to filter the view.
3. The table shows each player and their payment status for the selected month:
   - **Completed** — payment received
   - **Pending** — payment not yet made

> Coaches have a read-only view of payments. They cannot process payments on behalf of players.

---

## 5. Club Portal — Player

After logging in, players land on their **Player Dashboard**. The sidebar provides navigation to all player sections.

---

### 5.1 Dashboard & Performance Analytics

**What it shows:**

- **Current Form Rating** — your average performance rating (0–10) calculated from recent matches.
- **Season Goals** — total goals scored this season.
- **Matches Played** — total matches logged by your coach.
- **Radar Chart (Attribute Wheel)** — a visual breakdown of your six core attributes (each rated 0–100):
  - Pace
  - Shooting
  - Passing
  - Dribbling
  - Defending
  - Physical
- **Performance Trend Chart** — a line/area chart showing your match ratings over time, helping you see your form trajectory.

> These values are updated by your coach when match stats are recorded.

---

### 5.2 Announcements

Displays all announcements posted by your coach. The most recent announcement appears at the top.

---

### 5.3 Schedule

Shows upcoming matches and training sessions posted by your coach in a calendar view. Use this to stay up to date on fixtures and training dates.

---

### 5.4 Settings & Profile

**What you can update:**

- **Profile photo**
- **Full name**
- **Height** (cm) and **Weight** (kg)
- **Strong foot** (Left / Right / Both)
- **Password** — enter your current password and a new password to update it.

Click **Save Changes** after making any edits.

---

### 5.5 Payments

**What it does:** Manage your monthly team subscription fee (MYR 50.00/month).

**How to pay:**

1. Navigate to **Payment** in the sidebar.
2. Your payment history is displayed by month (status: Pending or Completed).
3. To pay for a pending month, click **Pay Now**.
4. You will be redirected to **Billplz**, a secure Malaysian payment gateway.
5. Complete the payment on the Billplz page.
6. Once successful, you will be redirected back to NUVRA and the payment status will update to **Completed**.

> **Supported payment methods** depend on what is enabled via Billplz (typically FPX online banking).

## 6. Community Portal — Community Player

After logging in to the Community Portal, players land on the **Game Feed**.

---

### 6.1 Browsing Games

The feed displays all available community games. Each game card shows:

- **Game title**
- **Venue**
- **Date and time**
- **Team names** (e.g., Team A vs Team B)
- **Available slots** per team
- **Price** per player
- **Status**: Open, Full, Cancelled, or Completed

Games marked **Full** have no remaining slots. Games marked **Open** are available to join.

---

### 6.2 Joining a Game

1. Click on a game card to view full details.
2. On the game detail page, you can see:
   - Full game description
   - Current roster for Team A and Team B
3. Click **Join Game**.
4. Select which team you want to join — **Team A** or **Team B**.
5. Confirm your booking.

> You cannot join a game you have already booked, and you cannot join a team that is already full.

After joining, your name will appear on the team roster for that game.

---

### 6.3 Submitting Payment

After joining a game, you need to submit payment to confirm your spot.

1. On the game detail page, you will see a **payment QR code** provided by the admin.
2. Use your mobile banking app to scan the QR code and complete the payment.
3. Take a **screenshot or photo** of your payment receipt.
4. Return to the game page and click **Submit Payment**.
5. Upload your receipt image.
6. Your booking status will update to **Payment Submitted** and will be confirmed by the admin.

---

### 6.4 Community Announcements

Navigate to **Announcements** in the community menu to view the latest news, updates, and notices from the community admin.

---

## 7. Community Portal — Community Admin

Community admins have access to all community player features, plus additional management tools.

---

### 7.1 Creating a Game

1. Navigate to **Admin → Create Game**.
2. Fill in the game details:
   - **Title** — name of the game/event
   - **Venue** — location of the game
   - **Date and Time**
   - **Team A Name** and **Team B Name**
   - **Max slots per team** (maximum number of players per side)
   - **Price per player** (in MYR)
   - **Description** — any additional information for players
   - **Payment QR Code** — upload a QR code image for players to scan and pay
3. Click **Create Game** — it will immediately appear on the community game feed.

---

### 7.2 Posting Announcements

1. Navigate to **Admin → Post Announcement**.
2. Enter a **title** and the announcement **body/content**.
3. Click **Post** — all community members will see it in the Announcements section.

You can also delete outdated announcements from the same screen.

---

### 7.3 Analytics Dashboard

Navigate to **Admin → Analytics** to view community-wide statistics:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Total Games Created | Total number of games created on the platform |
| Players Registered | Total number of registered community players |
| Monthly Revenue | Total game fees collected in the current month |
| Recent Activity | Live feed of new bookings and player registrations |

## 8. Payments Summary

| Payment Type | Portal | Amount | Gateway | Who Pays |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription | Club Portal | MYR 50.00 / month | Billplz (FPX) | Players |
| Game Fee | Community Portal | Set per game (variable) | QR Code (manual) | Community Players |

**Club Portal payments** are processed securely through **Billplz**, a certified Malaysian payment processor. Players are redirected to Billplz and returned to NUVRA after payment is completed.

**Community Portal payments** are made directly by scanning a QR code provided by the admin. Players then upload proof of payment for admin confirmation.

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I use the same account for both the Club Portal and the Community Portal?**  
A: No. The Club Portal and Community Portal have separate registration and login systems. You will need a separate account for each.

---

**Q: I registered as a player but can't access my dashboard. What should I do?**  
A: New players start with a **Pending** status and must wait for their coach to approve them. You will see a Waiting Room page until approval is granted. Contact your coach if you've been waiting a long time.

---

**Q: How do I change the team I belong to?**  
A: Team assignment is done during onboarding (initial registration). To change teams, contact your coach or the platform administrator.

---

**Q: My performance stats look wrong. Who do I contact?**  
A: Performance stats are entered by your coach. If you notice an error, please reach out directly to your coach to have it corrected.

---

**Q: A community game is showing as Full. Can I join a waitlist?**  
A: Currently, there is no waitlist feature. You can check back later in case a player cancels their booking, which may free up a slot.

---

**Q: I submitted my payment receipt for a community game but my booking is not confirmed. What should I do?**  
A: Payment confirmation is done manually by the community admin. Allow some time for the admin to review your receipt. If it has been an extended period, contact the community admin directly.

---

**Q: I forgot my Club Portal password. How do I reset it?**  
A: Click **Forgot Password** on the Club Portal login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

---

**Q: Can I log in to the Club Portal using Google?**  
A: Yes. Click **Continue with Google** on the login page. If it's your first time, you will be taken through the onboarding steps to set up your player profile.
